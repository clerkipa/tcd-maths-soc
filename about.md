# The Dublin University Mathematical Society
The student mathematical society was established in 1923 by a group of Trinity students including Nobel Laureate Ernest Walton. The purpose of the society is to provide events of mathematical interest for its members, and to encourage the study of mathematics.

## Our Committee

Our committee is elected every year at our AGM.
